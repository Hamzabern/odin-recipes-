Slow-Cooker Thai Chicken

Here's a true dump and go dinner. Made with cheap ingredients like chicken thighs and served over rice, Nicole calls the creamy coconut milk sauce "liquid gold.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a large bowl.
  3. Beat in the butter until smooth.
